Beni Ourain rugs originate from the Atlas Mountains of Morocco, woven by the Berber tribes. Traditionally, these textiles were crafted using wool from local sheep, resulting in durable yet soft floor coverings. Many interior designers have embraced Beni Ourain rugs for their unique patterns and natural materials, making them a popular choice for styling homes with Beni Ourain rugs.
